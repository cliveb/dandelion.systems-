We could be great together

We are a small and ambitious team from Greater Seattle, We work with clients emerging enterprises local and international to bring ideas to life from research to product through architecture and engineering crafted and thoughtful design solutions.

PHILOSOPHY

Designed together

We were founded on the strong belief that having a cohesive research across all mediums creates a better solution experience. This is why we design together.

Open & collaborative

Collaboration is the foundation of our creative process. Great work happens when we work together with our clients to find creative solutions through open communication. So let's talk!

Research first, design later

Great design starts with a great idea, that’s why we start the design process with our minds first. We solve communication problems with creative thinking and design strategy.

The perfect fit

Our holistic approach allows us to tailor a design strategy using the technlogies that are right for you. With openness and honestly we deliver what you need, not just what you want.

We get anticipatory systems

It’s a data world and we embrace it by crafting data driven solutions that make things simpler, smarter, more beautiful and more enjoyable. We build applications with technlogies that work, on small screens, big screens and everything in-between.

Details are in the design

We are passionate about the craft of design. We pursue the highest level of quality in every detail of our work; through expert research and execution, one pixel at a time.

Direct to you

Our small team means our clients have a direct relationship with the designers working on their project. There are no salespeople or middle-men - Who you see is who you get.

Inside, out

We believe in authentic design that converts an honest ethnography of how people use systems. We learn what your people stand for, and what they represent, an convet this to create trust and meaning in anticipatory applications.

We have fun!

We love what we do and we have fun doing it. We want to work on interesting projects that we can add value to and work with our clients, not for our clients, while having fun along the way.

PROCESS

  • Discover

  • Create

  • Develop

  • Refine

  • Launch

  • Grow

  • We start all projects by working to thoroughly understand the business and project objectives. We ask questions, we listen and take the time to understand what you are all about.

  • Taking the ideas established in the discovery stage and applying creative thinking that starts to visualise design concepts.

  • Now that we have the big idea and a sense of what it might look like, we then begin the process of design exploration and development to push the idea further.

  • We take our creative developments and craft them into refined design outcomes, coming full circle with the objectives set out in the beginning of the project.

  • It's ready for the world to see! We ensure everything is finalised, tested and perfected before we bring it to life.

  • We don't love you and leave you. We build ongoing creative partnerships with our clients to ensure their brand's grow and stay relevant.
